BUILT BY EXPLORERS, FOR EXPLORERS

It was never meant to become an app.

XPLOR began with six mates spending every weekend climbing summits, finding caves and wild camping around the UK.

“We just needed one place to tick everything off and see how our lists compared.”

At first we used the Notes app. Everyone had a different list, different names and different photos. There was no clear way to see who had completed what, where we'd camped, which caves we'd explored or what the group still had left to do.

We assumed an app already existed. We searched for hours and found navigation apps, route planners and weather tools, but nothing that solved the simple problem we actually had: put every location in one place, tick them off, see them visually on a map and compare progress with friends.

So XPLOR started as a basic private list for the six of us. Then we kept adding more. More locations. More progress. Profiles. Friends. Soon other people saw it and asked for access. Then their friends wanted in too.

What began as a tool for our weekends became the outdoor platform we couldn't find anywhere else.
